A World Heritage Site

Today, the Great Wall is recognized as one of the most impressive architectural feats in history. In 1987, it was
designated a UNESCO World Heritage site.

"The Great Wall of China is a symbol of the endurance and ingenuity of the Chinese people. "

Conservation Efforts

While some parts are well-preserved for tourists (like Badaling), other parts are crumbling
due to erosion and human activity. National laws now protect the wall from further damage.
