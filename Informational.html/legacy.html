In 1987, UNESCO protected the wall to ensure its survival for future generations.

"The wall is a testament to human perseverance."

🚫 Myth vs. Reality

Because the wall is so old and famous, many legends have appeared. Here are the facts:

The Myth The Reality
It can be seen from the Moon. False. It is only visible from low-Earth orbit under perfect conditions.
It is a single, continuous line. False. It is a network of walls, trenches, and natural barriers like mountains.
Bodies are buried inside the wall. Mostly False. While many died during work, there is no evidence they were used as "fill" for the wall.
