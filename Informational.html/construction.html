Materials and Labor

The wall was a massive logistical undertaking involving soldiers, peasants, and even prisoners.

The Main Dynasties

  1. Qin Dynasty: Used rammed earth and wood.
  2. Han Dynasty: Expanded the wall using sand and reeds.
  3. Ming Dynasty: Used heavy bricks and stone.

🏗️ The Builder's Secret: Rice?

One of the most fascinating parts of the Ming Dynasty wall is the mortar used to hold bricks together. Click below to reveal the secret ingredient:

Click to reveal the "Super Glue" of the 1400s

Engineers used Sticky Rice Flour! They mixed it with slaked lime to create a mortar so strong that weeds still can't grow between the bricks today.
It was one of the first composite mortars in history.
