An Ancient Wonder

The Great Wall is more than just a wall; it is a sprawling system of trenches, hills, and stone that stretches across the horizon of China.

Quick Facts

  • Length: 21,196 kilometers.
  • Age: Over 2,300 years old.
  • Visibility: Despite the myth, it is difficult to see from space without aid!

📍 Essential Visitor Info

If you are planning to see this wonder in person, keep these sections in mind:

Badaling
The most popular and well-preserved section. Very accessible from Beijing.
Mutianyu
Slightly less crowded, famous for its beautiful surrounding forest and a fun toboggan ride down!
Jiankou
Known as "The Wild Wall." It is steep, unrepaired, and for experienced hikers only.
