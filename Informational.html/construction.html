Materials and Labour

The wall was not built all at once. Different dynasties used different materials based on what was available nearby at the time.

The Main Dynasties

  1. Qin Dynasty: Used rammed earth and wood.
  2. Han Dynasty: Expanded the wall using sand and reeds.
  3. Ming Dynasty: This is the famous part we see today, built using heavy bricks and stone.

Defense Features

The wall featured watchtowers, troop barracks, and signaling stations using smoke and fire to communicate messages across vast distances.
